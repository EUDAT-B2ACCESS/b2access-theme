DPM

DPM is a tool that provides data policy management functionality. It can be used by data managers to define data policies, store them and share them with the data service providers, who will enforce them; service integrators to integrate a third-party service with the DPM relying on the XML DB API; B2SAFE service administrators to have a view on the policies defined by a community data manager -They can download and enforce them and provide feedback to the data managers.

DPMT

The DPMT is EUDAT CDIs e-Infrastructure registry for managing and distributing information about service providers, the providers' service and resource offers, the actually provided service instances (RegisteredServices) and the related service components (RegisteredServiceComponents). It obtains information about products offered by EUDAT from the EUDAT Service Catalog that is managed via the EUDAT Service Portfolio Management Tool (SPMT).
The DPMT compise a customer management data base in that the CDI customers (organisations and representing persons) and their business cases (data projects) are registered. The accounted usage of storage and compute resources are mapped to the data projects.
